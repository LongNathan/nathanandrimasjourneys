Why do we do this?

Nathan and Rima enjoy documenting their journeys together and to share their experiences in Russia (Where they currently are.) as an American and Filipina or elsewhere in the world. They both love to travel to new places and enjoy the local atmosphere and cuisine as well as get acquainted with the local customs and culture. The main reason why they like to share their experiences is to give other people an idea of what some of these other places have to offer to the rest of the world and to also share the culture and experiences of the people that they have visited. They have been to and have seen places all over the world from America all the way to Europe and Asia and there are many interesting sights and sounds that deserve to be recognized and praised by other people and the only way to get their stories across is by videoing it and sharing it to others on platforms such as YouTube. They are always happy and excited to be able to capture whatever life wants to throw at them and feel that they should always remain positive no matter what the outcome of it might possibly be. They may not always post weekly, but that is because of their personal lives do not always allow them to be able to document the realities of what is occurring around them at that moment, but they always try to find something interesting that can be shared and will always share it. So they hope that you will enjoy this ride of their journeys together with them and will always find happiness.

Nathan and Rima do their best with the equipment that they have and with your help they could get better equipment and software to make their videos with and to be able to go to different places and hopefully interview some people there. It would be greatly appreciated if you could support them in making their dreams come true. Your support could be as simple as subscribing to their channel and liking and sharing their videos and while it is not your responsibility to support us whatsoever any donations would be received with great thanks. If you do decide to donate to Nathan and Rima you would get credited in their videos with depending on how much you donate to them. You can donate to them by simply going to our Patreon page and subscribing by clicking on the “P” on the menu on the top or on the bottom of this webpage and decide which level you want to give your support and donation to. If you have a product that you want us to sponsor for you or your company, please click on the “Contact Us” button above and they will be happy to discuss the sponsorship with you. They hope that you will consider helping them in their journeys and their dreams by making this a full time job for them and they promise that you will not be disappointed.

Everyone has that spark and curiosity in them about traveling and exploring new places and cultures all the way from our ancestors leaving Africa many thousands of years ago to all the way to Neil Armstrong and all the other Apollo Moon astronaut explorers and at this moment in human exploration we are looking ever further off this planet and into the deepest darkest areas of space to explore and settle with Mars close on the horizon. It is within our genes to have an urge to learn more about the places we live in and the need to try anything and everything even if it brings great risk to ourselves and it is with that attitude of our ancestors that Nathan and Rima will bring to you. How about it? Will you join Nathan and Rima on this journey through life?

A Little Bit About Us

Nathan

Nathan was born in the Midwest of the United States. He enjoys traveling and blogging and that is why he along with Rima set up their YouTube channel for other people to enjoy the scenery. He also enjoys playing video games mainly fantasy games and R.P.G.s in general. Nathan also likes to listen to music of any type as well as watching Sci-Fi movies and TV shows. Whenever Nathan travels he likes to experience the local culture and cuisine so he can feel what it is like to live there even if for a short time. Nathan as always looks forward to hearing from you either on the website or from the YouTube channel.

Rima

Rima was born in Manila, Philippines and she has been all around Europe and Asia. She has been to Hong Kong and Malaysia as well as the Maldives. Rima now lives in Russia with Nathan and she has been here since 2007. While Rima has been in Russia she has built up for herself a community and an organization; an organization that is recognized by the Philippine embassy in Russia. She also likes to help out people when they have some sort of emergency. When Rima is not working she likes to spend time with Burnok and also watching Filipino shows on YouTube.
